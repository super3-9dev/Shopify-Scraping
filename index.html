Description

The Onyx Ring. Created using real cuts of a black Onyx stone. Every stone is unique making it the perfect Ring for your unique style. The Black onyx Ring is one of three stone Rings added to the Ring collection.

DETAILS

  • Hypoallergenic | No Tarnishing | No Fading | No Irritation
  • Natural Onyx Gemstone
  • Oval Signet
  • Materials: Made from Recycled Stainless Steel / 18K Gold PVD Plating
  • Included in our Lifetime Warranty
  • Packed in a Twistedpendant Jewellery Pouch

SIZE DETAILS

Available in US sizes 5–13:

  • 5–6 = XS

  • 7 = S

  • 8–9 = S/M

  • 10–11 = M/L

  • 12–13 = L/XL

Need help choosing a size? Our full chart includes exact measurements in mm.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

This ring is made from Recycled Stainless Steel. This ring will not tarnish, rust or turn your skin green. Safe to wear in water & is more durable than other materials commonly used such as Silver & Gold.
